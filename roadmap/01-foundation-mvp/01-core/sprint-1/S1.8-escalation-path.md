## Description

Implement escalation path: when confidence &lt; threshold, missing evidence, conflicting signals, or timeout, the agent produces an **escalation packet** (what we know, what we don’t, what to check). No silent failure. Goal F10 (Must).

---

## Requirements

- [x] Conditions for escalation: low confidence, no evidence, conflicting signals, timeout (and later: OPA error — S2).
- [x] When any condition holds: generate escalation packet (structured: what we know, what we don’t, what to check).
- [x] Escalation packet is part of the run output and/or report; no “agent was wrong, too bad” — clear handoff.
- [ ] Evals: at least one “must escalate” case (S1.11).
