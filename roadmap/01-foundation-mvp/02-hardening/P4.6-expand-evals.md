## Description

Expand the eval suite to roughly 20–30 cases; add citation precision (or MoE) into scoring where applicable. Reduces regressions and supports MoE1–MoE4 (goals.md §4.4). Roadmap: "Expand eval suite (e.g. 20–30 cases); citation precision / MoE in scoring."

---

## Requirements

- [ ] At least 20–30 eval cases total (triage, citation, escalation, injection).
- [ ] Citation precision (MoE2): evals flag or score when plan steps are not supported by citations (e.g. step references doc_id not in retrieved set).
- [ ] Triage accuracy (MoE1), unsafe-action rate = 0 (MoE3), escalation correctness (MoE4) remain covered.
- [ ] CI runs full suite; pass threshold or required checks documented.
- [ ] Cases are deterministic (pinned model/seed); CI stable.
